How It Works

From Send Click to Safe Delivery — in Four Steps

MailVeriQ sits between your email server and the outside world. Every outbound message is inspected before delivery. See the dashboard →

1

Your User Hits Send

The email is transparently routed through the MailVeriQ Gateway before leaving your environment. Encrypted connections ensure data is secure in transit.

2

MailVeriQ Inspects

The body and attachments are scanned against pre-built and custom patterns for financial data, health records, personal identifiers, and confidential business information.

3

Policies Decide

Clean emails go out instantly; risky emails are held in quarantine and assigned to the right reviewers according to your approval chain and SLAs.

4

Every Decision Recorded

Approvals, rejections, reviewer comments, and timestamps are captured in an immutable ledger — ready for audits, forensics, or internal reporting at any time.
