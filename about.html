About MailVeriQ

Securing outbound email. Preventing data loss.

Our Mission

MailVeriQ Technologies was founded with a singular purpose: to eliminate the risk of sensitive data leaving organizations through outbound email. While the industry focused on inbound threats — malware, phishing, spam — we recognized that the most damaging breaches often originate from within. A misaddressed email containing patient records, an accidental attachment with financial data, or an employee exfiltrating intellectual property can cost millions in regulatory fines, legal liability, and reputational harm.

We built MailVeriQ Gateway to give security teams full visibility and control over outbound email, without disrupting the people who rely on email to do their jobs every day.

What We Do

MailVeriQ Gateway is an enterprise-grade email Data Loss Prevention (DLP) platform deployed as a SaaS SMTP gateway. Every outbound email passes through our inspection engine, where configurable policies scan for sensitive data patterns — credit card numbers, Social Security numbers, protected health information, personally identifiable information, and custom patterns defined by your organization.

Flagged messages are quarantined and routed through multi-level approval workflows. Authorized reviewers can approve, reject, or redact content before release. Every action is logged in an immutable audit trail, providing the evidence organizations need for compliance with HIPAA, GDPR, PCI DSS, SOX, and other regulatory frameworks.

The platform is multi-tenant by design, with database-level row isolation, role-based access control across nine granular permission levels, and infrastructure certified to ISO 27001 and SOC 2 standards.
