Join Our Team

Build the future of outbound email security.

At MailVeriQ, every team member plays a direct role in protecting organizations from data loss. We are a remote-first, security-focused company where engineers, product leaders, and customer advocates work together to solve one of the most persistent challenges in enterprise security: preventing sensitive data from leaving through outbound email.

We move fast, hold ourselves to high standards, and measure success by the real-world impact we deliver for our customers. If you thrive in an environment where your work genuinely matters, we want to hear from you.

Our Culture

  • Remote-First: Our team is distributed across multiple time zones. We hire the best talent regardless of location and invest in the tools, processes, and rituals that make distributed collaboration effective.
  • Security-Focused: Security is not just our product — it is how we operate. Every team member completes security awareness training, and our internal practices meet the same standards we hold for our platform.
  • Impact-Driven: We prioritize outcomes over activity. Quarterly objectives are tied to customer impact, and every team has visibility into how their work contributes to the company's mission.
  • Continuous Learning: The threat landscape never stands still, and neither do we. We sponsor conference attendance, provide learning stipends, and dedicate time for research and experimentation.

Open Positions

Senior Security Engineer

Remote Full-time Engineering

Design and implement advanced detection patterns for sensitive data in email content and attachments. Work on our core DLP scanning engine, develop new content inspection capabilities, and conduct security research to stay ahead of emerging exfiltration techniques. Requires 5+ years in application security or DLP engineering.

Full Stack Developer

Remote Full-time Engineering

Build and maintain the MailVeriQ management console, approval workflow interfaces, and reporting dashboards. Work across our React frontend and Node.js backend, with deep integration into PostgreSQL and our multi-tenant architecture. Requires 3+ years of full stack experience with modern JavaScript frameworks.

Product Manager

Remote Full-time Product

Own the product roadmap for MailVeriQ Gateway. Conduct customer research, define requirements, and prioritize features that deliver measurable security outcomes. Partner closely with engineering and customer success to translate complex compliance requirements into intuitive platform capabilities. Requires 4+ years in B2B SaaS product management, preferably in security or compliance.

Customer Success Manager

Remote Full-time Customer Success

Guide enterprise customers from onboarding through ongoing optimization of their email DLP policies. Serve as the primary point of contact for a portfolio of accounts, conduct quarterly business reviews, and identify opportunities to expand platform adoption. Requires 3+ years in customer success or technical account management within enterprise security or SaaS.

DevOps Engineer

Remote Full-time Engineering

Own the infrastructure that powers MailVeriQ Gateway — from CI/CD pipelines and container orchestration to monitoring, incident response, and capacity planning. Maintain our AWS-based infrastructure with a focus on reliability, security, and cost efficiency. Requires 4+ years in DevOps or SRE roles with strong AWS and Kubernetes experience.
